# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

This project exists to make firmware iteration faster. Contributions are judged on technical merit. Expected behavior:

- Be direct and constructive in code review
- Respect that contributors may be working across different toolchains, boards, and constraints
- Accept that maintainers may close issues or PRs that don't fit the project's scope

Not acceptable:

- Personal attacks or harassment
- Dismissing issues based on who filed them rather than their content
- Spam, off-topic promotion, or low-effort noise

## Scope

These standards apply in GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and any project-related communication.

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported to gauthier.lechevalier26@gmail.com. Reports are handled confidentially. Responses may include a warning, temporary restriction, or permanent ban depending on severity.
